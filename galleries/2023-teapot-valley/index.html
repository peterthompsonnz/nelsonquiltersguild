Teapot Valley Winter Retreat

The annual Teapot Valley Winter retreat was held over the weekend of the 9th to the 11th of June. The weather was sunny and crisp, and there was a good turn out of quilters. One of whom, Debbie Williams, came from afar afield as Queenstown. It was good that she did as her applique work wowed those present with its wonderfull creativity.
