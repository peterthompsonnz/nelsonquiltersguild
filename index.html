Welcome

Nelson (New Zealand) Quilters was established in 1983 as the Sun City Patchwork and Quilters. Since that time it has continued to grow. There are presently about 80 members and at most monthly meetings we have around 40 quilters present. Click the button below to view/download the membership application form.

Information/Application Form
To get an idea about the quilts that our members make, and the workshops and retreats that are held, have a look at the Galleries page.

Meetings
