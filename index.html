Welcome

The Nelson (New Zealand) Quilters' Guild was established in 1983 as the Sun City Patchwork and Quilters. Since that time it has continued to grow. The Guild has 80 members and at most monthly meetings we have about 40 quilters present. Click the button below to view/download the guild's application form.

Information/Application Form

Meetings

When

Meetings are held on the 3rd Saturday of every month from February to November. They usually run from 10am to 2pm.

Where

Woodstock Centre (school hall)
Stoke School
Main Road Stoke
Nelson

There is car parking in the school grounds. Visitors are most welcome

Members' Benefits

These include

  • Social meetings with refreshments
  • Speakers
  • Library
  • Raffle
  • Subsidised workshops and retreats

Subscriptions

  • $35 p.a.
  • $16 fee for name badge

Contact

Please note that the guild is based in Nelson, New Zealand
