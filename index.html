Welcome

The Nelson Quilters' Guild was established in 1983 as the Sun City Patchwork and Quilters. Since that time it has continued to grow. The Guild has 80 members and at most monthly meetings we have about 40 quilters present.

Become a Guild Member

Meetings

When

  • 7.30pm on the 3rd Wednesday of each month [except for June and July]
  • June & July day meetings on the Saturday following the 3rd Wednesday from 10am-2pm
  • There is no club activity in January

Where

  • Woodstock Centre
  • Stoke School
  • Main Road Stoke
  • Nelson
  • There is car parking in the school grounds. Visitors are most welcome

Members' Benefits

These include

  • Social meetings with refreshments
  • Speakers
  • Library
  • Raffle
  • Subsidised workshops and retreats

Subscriptions

  • $35 p.a.
  • $16 fee for name badge
